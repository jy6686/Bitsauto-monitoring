Assessment of the Three New Additions

This is the strongest addition.

safe zone

visually.

That reduces cognitive load dramatically during incidents.

The choice to derive capacity dynamically from observed peak + headroom was also correct for Phase 1.

You avoided hardcoding carrier/network assumptions.

2. Connected Ratio Glow ✓✓

Very strong.

Because now the graph card itself becomes a health signal.

That creates:

peripheral awareness

Operators can detect degradation from the edge of their vision.

That’s how mature NOC systems behave.

The smooth transition timing (0.6s) was also the right choice.

No flashing.
No panic UI.

3. Surge Detection Badge ✓

Good addition.

CPS spikes

sudden reconnect storms

before incidents formally open.

3. Quality Pressure Correlation

This is the next truly intelligent layer.

Example:

ASR declining

capacity exhaustion or routing instability
