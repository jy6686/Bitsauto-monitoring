pending status would be  considered as blocked to send traffic 

I would not make Pending mean "blocked to send traffic" in the routing sense.

There are actually three different concepts that should remain separate:

Status	Commercial Use	Send Rate	Routing Traffic
Approved	✅ Yes	✅ Yes	Depends on client tariff
Pending	❌ No	❌ No	Existing traffic unaffected
Blocked	❌ No	❌ No	Can be explicitly prevented
Why?

Imagine today:

Pakistan Ufone Mobile
Status = Approved

100 clients already have rates and live traffic.

Tomorrow you decide to review destinations and accidentally bulk change everything to:

Status = Pending

You do not want:

Live Traffic Stops

That would be a major operational outage.

✓ Send Rate
✓ Product Rates
✓ Customer Pricing
✓ Rate Sheets
Pending
Hidden from:
✗ Send Rate
✗ Product Pricing
✗ New Customer Offers

Existing client tariffs remain untouched
Existing traffic continues

Hidden from commercial modules
Flagged as restricted
