what is our product policy for rate manager, product catelogue 

Based on all the decisions we've made, the Product Policy should be very clear because this is the foundation for Billing, Rates, Margin, Invoicing, and Commercial Identity.

Product Policy — BitsAuto
Principle #1: Products are Commercial Classes, Not Destinations

Products:

FC = First Class
BC = Business Class
SB = Standard Business
SC = Super Class

Products are pricing/routing strategies, not prefixes.

Wrong
1923 Pakistan
2923 Pakistan
6923 Pakistan
7923 Pakistan
Correct
923 Pakistan Mobile

with:

FC Rate = 0.0230
BC Rate = 0.0194
SB Rate = 0.0320
SC Rate = 0.0400

6 SB

Final Rule

Customers buy destinations.
Operations manage products.

So:

Customer sees:

FC | BC | SB | SC

That separation should be enforced across Product Catalog, Destination Catalog, Rate Manager, Billing, Invoicing, and future Rate Sheet exports.
