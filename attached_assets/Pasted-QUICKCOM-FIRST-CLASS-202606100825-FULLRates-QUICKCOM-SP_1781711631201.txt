What I Have Learned From All Screens So Far

The legacy BitsAuto commercial workflow had five major layers:

Layer 1

Rate Repository

Layer 2

Client Tariff Profiles

(what client receives)

Layer 3

Rate Sheet Templates

(how client receives)

Layer 4

Client Validation Rules

(whether client should receive)

Layer 5

Notification & Delivery

(send/download/email)

This validation layer is actually more important than the PDF generator because it controls commercial governance and prevents bad rate changes from being distributed automatically.

At this point, the old system architecture is becoming very clear: it wasn't just a rate sender—it was a complete Commercial Rate Governance Platform sitting between supplier rate updates and customer tariff publication.
