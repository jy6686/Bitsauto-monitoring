The schema already has the billing cycle and invoice email fields — the foundation for automatic dispatch is there. The gap is that nothing orchestrates them into a scheduled pipeline.

Volume 2 — Immediate (builds on what exists)

1. Automatic Invoice Dispatch Engine (highest value, most requested)

The clientBillingCycle and invoiceEmail columns exist but nothing reads them on a schedule. The engine would:

Nightly cron job checks which companies have a billing cycle closing today
Pulls CDRs for that period from the existing CDR cache + /asr_acd.php aggregation
Generates PDF via the existing invoice generator
Emails it to invoiceEmail with CDR CSV attached
Logs delivery status (sent / failed / pending approval)
This is the "1st to send invoice to client with reconciliation with Sippy data/CDR" item the document flags as top priority.

2. Vendor Invoice Reconciliation

The billingDisputes table already exists. The missing piece is the comparison engine:

Import vendor invoice (CSV or manual entry)
Compare against platform CDRs for same period
Surface duration mismatches, missing calls, cost variance per destination
Auto-create dispute record when variance exceeds threshold
Approval workflow to accept/reject/escalate
3. Real-Time Margin Intelligence (expand what exists)

Revenue analytics are already on the dashboard. Expand to show:

Margin per carrier, per destination, per client — live
Alert when route margin drops below configured threshold
"Negative margin detected" warnings before invoice is sent
Volume 3 — High Value (after Volume 2 is stable)

4. Credit Control Engine — integrates with the existing creditLimit field, paymentTerm, and the provisioning/routing layer. Auto-block when balance threshold is hit.

5. Customer Profitability Intelligence — per-client P&L combining revenue (from CDRs), routing cost (vendor CDRs), and fraud exposure (from the fraud engine). Answers "which clients are actually profitable after cost and fraud."

6. Billing Governance Workflow — extends existing approval infrastructure: Generated → Finance Review → Approved → Locked → Sent → Paid → Archived. Finance role sees approval queue.

7. Revenue Leakage Detection — scans for unrated traffic, zero-cost vendor calls, tariff mismatches, duplicate CDR rows. Flags them before invoice is locked.

Do not build yet (Volume 4+):

AI/ML billing anomaly models
Predictive churn-linked revenue forecasting
Autonomous vendor dispute resolution
Closed-loop financial optimization

CDRs
→ Rating Engine
→ Snapshot Layer
→ Invoice Objects
