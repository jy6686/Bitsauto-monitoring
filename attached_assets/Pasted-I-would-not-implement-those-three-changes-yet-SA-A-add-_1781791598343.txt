What concerns me

The proposed change:

SA → A

is risky.

Your own Sippy template shows:

Action [A|D|U|S|SA]

Historically in Sippy:

A = Add
U = Update
D = Delete
SA = Add or Update (safe upsert)

For an existing prefix like:

19230 = 0.0275

switching from SA to A may actually make things worse because A may refuse existing rows.

I would not change action codes until we see the actual upload result.

The bigger clue

Your screenshot of Sippy tariff page shows:

Upload Rates
Format: .csv or MS-Office 2007 .xlsx

and

Select File

There is no indication that Sippy requires:

rate_file

or

upload_file

or

i_tariff in POST body

Those are assumptions.
