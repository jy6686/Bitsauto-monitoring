Recommendation for BitsAuto

Do not use account names, customer_product_assignments, or tariff-sync logic for initial client rate governance.

Use:

Client Wizard
    ↓
Selected Product(s)
    ↓
Create Initial Client Rate Job
    ↓
KAM assigns initial rates
    ↓
Approval
    ↓
Send Rate Notification
    ↓
Activate

This matches how the legacy BitsAuto commercial process already works and keeps product ownership with Commercial/KAM instead of trying to infer it from Sippy tariffs.
