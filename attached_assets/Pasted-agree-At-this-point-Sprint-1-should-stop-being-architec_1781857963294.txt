Where the Two Reviews Differ
Per-Destination Effective Date

My earlier suggestion: each queue row gets its own date/time
Your review: disagree for Phase 1 — real wholesale carriers use one effective date per batch. Per-row override is Phase 3 at earliest, and only if the business actually needs it

Job Queue / Background Worker timing

My earlier suggestion: design the async job queue as Phase 1
