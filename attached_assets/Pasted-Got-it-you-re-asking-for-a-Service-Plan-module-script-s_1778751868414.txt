🧩 1. SERVICE PLAN — WHAT IT IS (CORE IDEA)

A Service Plan in your platform =

A rule-set that controls how a client or vendor is billed + routed + limited in Sippy.

It sits between:

Client / Vendor Account
Rate Plan / Routing Group
Billing Engine / Finance
