I agree. P2.5 — Call Identity Audit is probably the highest-leverage diagnostic step before any major CCI work.

Why this is important

Right now the team is discussing building:

Commercial Call Identity (CCI)

But before building a new identity layer, we should answer:

What identity already exists?

In telecom systems, it's very common that the answer is already available in one of:

SIP Call-ID
Sippy Conf-ID
Asterisk UniqueID
Acct Session ID
Orig Call-ID

If one of those already appears across:

Customer CDR
Vendor CDR
P&L

then the reconciliation problem becomes dramatically simpler.
