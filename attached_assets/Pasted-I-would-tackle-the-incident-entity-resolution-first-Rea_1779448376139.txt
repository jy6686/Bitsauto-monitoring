I would tackle the incident entity resolution first.

Reason:
it is currently the single most visible credibility gap in the operational layer.

From the operator perspective:

[HIGH] Incident — PLATFORM: ASR anomaly — unknown entity

immediately creates doubt:

“Does the system actually know what failed?”
“Can I trust the RCA later?”
“Is correlation really working?”

Even though the telemetry underneath is correct.

The important thing is:
this is NOT a telemetry problem anymore.

It is:

signal attribution
entity enrichment
incident presentation

That makes it:

contained scope
low architectural risk
high operator trust impact

Exactly the kind of refinement worth doing now.

Why I Would Do This Before NER

NER is valuable, but:

it depends on validating CDR fidelity first
it may expose ingestion limitations
it may require schema enrichment

That is an investigation gate first, not an immediate operational win.

Meanwhile:
incident entity resolution is:

already possible
immediately visible
immediately improves NOC Command quality
immediately improves escalation readability
immediately improves future RCA usefulness
Recommended Resolution Strategy

At incident creation time:

Resolve and persist:

vendor name
client name
country
destination
routing group
switch
correlation source

from:

concurrent snapshots
carrier scoring
SIP OPTIONS failures
CDR aggregates
anomaly payload context

before title generation.

Recommended Incident Title Pattern

Instead of:

[HIGH] Incident — PLATFORM: ASR anomaly — unknown entity

Use:

[HIGH] Vendor: Callntalk — ASR degradation (Bangladesh Mobile)

or:

[CRITICAL] Client: internal-bd — Concurrent session collapse

That instantly makes the NOC screen feel:

operational
actionable
trustworthy
Then After That

Proceed with:

CDR disposition field investigation
NER feasibility validation
ACD/FAS overlays
RCA summaries

That sequence becomes cleaner because incidents will already have trustworthy entity attribution.

My Recommended Immediate Priority
Priority	Item	Reason
1	Incident entity resolution	highest visible trust gain
2	Validate disposition fields	NER gate
3	ACD/FAS overlays	low-risk operational enrichment
4	RCA summaries	major maturity jump

That order gives the best balance of:

operator trust
low risk
visible progress
architectural cleanliness.
