One important recommendation

Do NOT use heavy 3D/WebGL yet.

Stick with:

SVG
lightweight animations
Framer Motion
React Simple Maps or D3 SVG

Why:

stable on wallboards
lower GPU load
smoother on Replit
easier future scaling
cleaner responsive behavior

This is the right production decision for telecom dashboards.
