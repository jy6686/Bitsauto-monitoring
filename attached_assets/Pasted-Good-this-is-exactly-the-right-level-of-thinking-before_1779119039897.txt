🧭 What actually happens when you move to a custom domain (truth layer)
1. Nothing changes in your operations model

A custom domain on Replit is:

DNS → points to Replit deployment
Replit → still runs your server, DB, logs, deploys

So:

You still debug in Replit
You still deploy from Replit
You still see logs in Replit
You still fix code in Replit

👉 You are not “moving off Replit” — you are only masking the URL.

Before:
https://vo-ip-watcher--junaid70.replit.app/portal/xxx
After:
https://portal.bitsauto.com/portal/xxx

That’s it.

Everything else is identical.
