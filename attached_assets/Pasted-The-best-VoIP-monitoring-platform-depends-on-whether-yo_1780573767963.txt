The best VoIP monitoring platform depends on whether you’re a carrier, enterprise, or VoIP service provider. For telecom operators and wholesale voice businesses, these are among the strongest options:

Top VoIP Monitoring Platforms

1. ** VoIPmonitor￼**
    * Purpose-built for SIP and RTP monitoring
    * MOS score calculation
    * Call recording and troubleshooting
    * Packet loss, jitter, and latency analysis
    * Excellent for wholesale voice carriers
2. ** Homer SIP Capture (HEP/HOMER)￼**
    * Open-source
    * Deep SIP tracing and troubleshooting
    * Widely used by telecom engineers
    * Integrates with SBCs and softswitches
3. ** IR Prognosis￼**
    * Enterprise-grade monitoring
    * Real-time voice quality analytics
    * Strong alerting and reporting
    * Used by large operators and enterprises
4. ** Nectar Services￼**
    * End-to-end UC and VoIP monitoring
    * SIP, Teams, Cisco, Avaya support
    * Quality and user experience monitoring
5. ** Grafana￼ + Prometheus**
    * Flexible and cost-effective
    * Custom dashboards for ASR, ACD, PDD, CPS, NER
    * Ideal if you have in-house development resources

For Wholesale Voice Carriers

If you’re running international voice traffic and carrier interconnections, I would rank them:

1. VoIPmonitor
2. Homer SIP Capture
3. Grafana + Prometheus
4. IR Prognosis

Key Metrics to Monitor

* ASR (Answer Seizure Ratio)
* ACD (Average Call Duration)
* PDD (Post Dial Delay)
* CPS (Calls Per Second)
* Jitter
* Packet Loss
* Latency
* SIP Error Codes (403, 404, 480, 486, 503, etc.)
* Carrier-specific quality trends

For a setup involving softswitches such as Sippy, PortaOne, Class 4/5 switches, or SBCs, a combination of VoIPmonitor + Grafana is often considered one of the most effective and cost-efficient solutions.

For a normal telecom company, that ranking is reasonable.
