Priority 2 — Financial Truth Layer

This is more important than Auto Margin Fix.

Today:

Vendor uploaded rate
↓
Rate pushed to Sippy
↓
Calls routed
↓
Invoices generated

But nobody verifies:

Pushed Rate
=
Actual Rate Used
=
Actual Customer Billing
What should happen

For every destination:

Vendor Cost
↓
Sippy Tariff
↓
CDR Billing
↓
Invoice

Compare all four.

Alert if:

Vendor Cost: $0.050
Sippy Tariff: $0.055
Customer Billed: $0.040

This identifies:

margin leakage
wrong tariff assignments
stale rates
billing disputes

This is extremely valuable for finance.

Priority 5 — Autonomous Rate Push

I would not fully automate this yet.

Instead:

Semi-Automatic
Vendor Rate Upload
↓
Analyze Impact
↓
Show Diff
↓
Approve
↓
Push to Sippy

Much safer.

Fully automatic pushes can accidentally:

destroy margins
overwrite client pricing
create billing disputes

Especially in wholesale voice.
