First — Important Clarification About WhatsApp

You asked:

what we need to integrate with WhatsApp to terminate SMS/Voice OTP

Architecturally:

WhatsApp does NOT terminate SIP or SMS

WhatsApp becomes:

another delivery channel

inside BitsAuto orchestration.

Correct flow:

OTP Request
   ↓
BitsAuto Policy Engine
   ├ SMS via REVE
   ├ Voice via Sippy
   └ WhatsApp via UltraMsg/Meta

So:

REVE does NOT manage WhatsApp
Sippy does NOT manage WhatsApp
BitsAuto orchestrates all three

That separation is VERY important.
