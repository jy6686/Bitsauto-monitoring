After reading your findings, I would actually change the recommendation.

For your environment, I would NOT build approvals at all and I would be very careful with full automation.

The Real Problem Is Not Uploading

The real problem is:

Vendor Rate Sheet
      ↓
Which Vendor?
      ↓
Which Product?
      ↓
Which Internal Route Class?
      ↓
Which Sippy Destination Set?
      ↓
Push

The dangerous step is not uploading the file.

The dangerous step is selecting the wrong destination set.

What I See As The Biggest Risk

Today:

Upload Vendor Sheet
      ↓
Push To Destination Set
      ↓
User chooses DestSetID

This is where mistakes happen.

Example:

Vendor: ABC

Gold Sheet

Operator accidentally selects:

OPEN RTP DestSet

instead of:

Premium DestSet

Now 2,500 routes are pushed into the wrong routing class.

That is a much bigger risk than missing approvals.
