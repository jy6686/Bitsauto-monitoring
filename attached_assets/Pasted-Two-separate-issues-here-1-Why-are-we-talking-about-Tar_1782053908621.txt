Two separate issues here:

1. Why are we talking about Tariff 33?

Tariff 33 is not a fixed system tariff.

It is simply the tariff assigned to the specific customer/account you tested (internal-ptcl).

From your successful test:

New destination: prefix 29233 added to tariff 33

That means:

internal-ptcl
   ↓
Sippy Customer
   ↓
Assigned Tariff ID = 33

The system should never hardcode tariff 33.

Correct flow is:

Selected Client
    ↓
Find customer's assigned tariff
    ↓
Push rate into THAT tariff

For another customer it could be:

Tariff 28
Tariff 41
Tariff 55
Tariff 102

depending on what service plan/tariff is assigned in Sippy.

So if anybody says:

"Push to tariff 33"

that is only valid for the client tested, not for the entire platform.
