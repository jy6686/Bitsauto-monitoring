P2-D — Confidence & Provenance Governance

This is the MOST important part.

Every derived metric should carry:

Field	Purpose
provenance	native / derived
coverageRatio	sample coverage
confidence	high / medium / low
sampleSize	enrichment sample count
staleAge	telemetry age

Example:

FAS: 3.2%
Derived · Medium confidence
4,200 sampled calls / 20,232 total

That is enterprise-grade operational telemetry.
