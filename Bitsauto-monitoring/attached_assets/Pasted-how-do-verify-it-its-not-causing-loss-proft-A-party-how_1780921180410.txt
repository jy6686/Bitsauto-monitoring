What to compare in Sippy CDRs
For each governed test call, find two CDR records in Sippy:

CDR	What it shows	Where to find
Customer CDR (A-leg)	Duration Sippy billed to your client	Sippy → Customers → CDR tab
Vendor CDR (B-leg)	Duration the carrier billed you	Sippy → Vendors → CDR tab
Match them by CLI + CLD + approximate call time.

The math
Revenue  = customer_duration × your_rate_to_client
Cost     = vendor_duration   × carrier_rate_to_you
Margin   = Revenue − Cost

No governance (baseline): both durations are the same (e.g. 180s). Margin = normal.

With governance (20s cap + 8s cleanup):

Customer billed: ~28s (Sippy stops when A-leg gets BYE at T+28s)
Vendor charged: ~28s (carrier gets BYE at T+28s via our AMI hangup)
Margin ratio: same as baseline — both are cut equally
Where it could go wrong (loss scenarios)
Scenario	Result
Vendor has minimum billing (e.g. 30s) and customer was only billed 10s	You pay 30s, collect 10s → loss
Sippy bills customer for full duration before redirect	Customer billed 180s, vendor 28s → profit (good)
Sippy cascades BYE to customer before your redirect takes effect	Customer billed 0s, vendor 28s → loss

If customer seconds < vendor seconds → investigate minimum billing or cascade timing
