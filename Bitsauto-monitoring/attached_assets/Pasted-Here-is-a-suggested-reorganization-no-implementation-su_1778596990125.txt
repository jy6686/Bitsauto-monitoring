Here is a suggested reorganization — no implementation, suggestion only:

Current problems:

"Intelligence" and "Troubleshooting" overlap (SIP Trace, RTP Analytics, Replay Engine are diagnostic tools, not intelligence)
Security and Finance are unrelated concepts forced together
DID Management, Rate Cards, and Products are buried in Platform despite being operational resources
Test Suite and Call Flow Sim are in unrelated groups despite both being diagnostic/testing tools
Tools is in Platform despite being a troubleshooting utility
Proposed structure — 9 groups:

1. Live Operations (unchanged)

Live Calls, Alerts, SBC Monitor, NOC View, Multi-Switch View, AI Ops Center

2. Client Operations (add DID Management — it's a client resource)

Rate Plan, Accounts, Balance, Client Portal, BitsEye, Traffic Map, DID Management ← moved from Platform

3. Vendor Operations (remove Test Suite — moves to Troubleshooting)

Vendors, Connections, Destination Sets, Routing Groups

4. Routing & LCR (add Carrier Scoring — it's routing intelligence, not general intelligence)

Routing Manager, LCR Analyser, Self-Heal Routes, Approval Queue, Carrier Scoring ← moved from Intelligence

5. Analytics & Reporting (rename from "Analytics")

Revenue Analytics, CDR Viewer, Graphs, QoS Heatmap, Reports

6. Troubleshooting ← NEW GROUP

SIP Trace Viewer (from Intelligence)
RTP Analytics (from Intelligence)
Replay Engine (from Intelligence)
Server Monitoring (from Intelligence)
Network Topology (from Intelligence)
Number Intelligence (from Intelligence)
Test Suite (from Vendor Operations)
Call Flow Simulator (from Platform)
Tools (from Platform)

7. Security & Compliance ← (renamed + split from "Security & Finance")

Fraud / FAS, STIR/SHAKEN, Call Recordings, SLA Management, Firewall Manager, Compliance

8. Finance & Billing ← NEW GROUP (split from Security & Finance)

Cost Optimisation, Billing Disputes, Rate Cards (from Platform), Products (from Platform)

9. Platform (slimmed down — configuration only)

Settings, Team & KAM, Reseller Mgmt, API Keys, VPN Config, Notifications, SMS/A2P, Sidebar Menu

Summary of moves:

Item	From	To
DID Management	Platform	Client Operations
Carrier Scoring	Intelligence	Routing & LCR
SIP Trace, RTP Analytics, Replay Engine, Network Topology, Server Monitoring, Number Intel	Intelligence	Troubleshooting
Test Suite	Vendor Operations	Troubleshooting
Call Flow Simulator, Tools	Platform	Troubleshooting
Rate Cards, Products	Platform	Finance & Billing
Cost Optimisation, Billing Disputes	Security & Finance	Finance & Billing
Fraud, Compliance, Firewall etc.	Security & Finance	Security & Compliance
