Your direction is correct. For this kind of telecom/NOC platform, the best approach is:

80% modern 2D motion
20% focused 3D visualization

That gives the app a premium “AI telecom operations center” feel without slowing operators down.

Best 2D Effects To Implement First
1. AI Ops Live Incident Animation

Use framer-motion.

Effect:
New incident card slides in
Severity badge pulses briefly
Resolved incidents fade/shrink out
Count numbers animate upward
Why it works:

Makes the feed feel alive and real-time.

2. Live Pulse Indicators

Pure CSS/Tailwind.

Add pulse effects to:
LIVE traffic
Active calls
Scheduler running
Carrier healthy/degraded state
AI engine status
Example look:
Green soft pulse = healthy
Amber wave = warning
Red fast pulse = incident

Very low cost, very high visual value.

3. Animated SIP Ladder Flow

This is the strongest functional animation in your entire platform.

Current:

Static SIP arrows

Upgrade:
INVITE arrow draws left → right
180 Ringing animates back
200 OK glows green
Failure codes flash red
Packet ripple effect on delay

This makes troubleshooting visually intuitive.

4. Animated Metric Cards

For:

ASR
PDD
MOS
Stability score
Concurrent calls
Effects:
Count-up animation
Smooth progress bars
Color transition on degradation
Mini sparkline motion

This instantly modernizes dashboards.

5. Route Decision Replay Animation

One of the coolest additions possible.

Example:

LCR Engine
   ↓
Carrier A rejected (486)
   ↓
Fallback triggered
   ↓
Carrier B accepted

Animate each step sequentially.

This makes routing intelligence understandable in seconds.

Best 3D Features (Only Specific Areas)
1. 3D Network Topology Map

Best use of 3D in this app.

Stack:
react-three-fiber
three.js
Show:
Carriers as nodes
Routes as glowing connections
Traffic volume = line thickness
Incidents = red pulses
Real-time route flow animation

This becomes your signature “NOC wall” page.

2. 3D Globe (Optional)

Very impressive visually.

Show:
Global traffic paths
Live call routes
Country incidents
Carrier hotspots

Good for demos, enterprise presentations, and executive dashboards.

3. Particle Signal Background

Subtle animated background:

Floating telecom signal particles
Connection waves
Neural-network style lines

Should stay low-opacity behind dashboards.

NOC operators need stability and readability first.
