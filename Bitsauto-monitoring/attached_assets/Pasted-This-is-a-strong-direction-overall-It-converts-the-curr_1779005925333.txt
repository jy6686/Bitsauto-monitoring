You are turning:
raw route statistics

2. Health Score Formula

The logic is good, but I would slightly improve it.

Current:

ASR 40%
ACD 30%
PDD 20%
Volume confidence 10%

Recommended refinement:

ASR = 35%
ACD = 30%
PDD = 15%
Failure streak penalty = 10%
Volume confidence = 10%

Reason:
A route with:

2 successful calls
100% ASR
3 min ACD

should NOT outrank:

500 stable calls
65% ASR
2.5 min ACD

You need confidence weighting.

Recommended Confidence Formula

Instead of fixed 10%, use logarithmic scaling:

confidence = min(1, log10(totalCalls + 1) / 2)

This avoids tiny sample sizes dominating rankings.

Excellent operational design.

3. FAS / Short Duration Detector

This is extremely valuable.

Your detection logic is operationally correct:

ASR > 50%
AND
ACD < 15s

That is classic:

fake answer supervision
dead air fraud
false connect signaling
billing manipulation

Example from your table:

92.68% ASR
00:09 ACD

That is suspicious immediately.

Recommended Enhancement

Add severity tiers:

Severity	Logic
Low	ASR > 50 AND ACD < 20s
Medium	ASR > 70 AND ACD < 15s
Critical	ASR > 85 AND ACD < 10s

This allows:

AI Ops prioritization
alert throttling
approval workflows

5. Dynamic Vendor Ranking

Very good feature.

The important insight:
Static route order is obsolete.

Telecom routing quality is time-sensitive.

A carrier that was:

excellent 1 hour ago
can become:
unusable now

Rolling windows solve this.

Recommended Window Strategy

Use 3 rolling windows:

Window	Purpose
5 min	Real-time degradation
15 min	Operational ranking
24 hour	Stability baseline

Then calculate:

RealtimeScore =
(15m score * 0.6)
+
(5m score * 0.3)
+
(24h score * 0.1)

This prevents:

short spikes
ranking instability
oscillation
