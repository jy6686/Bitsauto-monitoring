This is a cascading drop-down menu (also commonly referred to as a nested drop-down menu or a sub-menu).
Here is how it breaks down based on the image:
 Primary Level (Top Nav): Hovering over or clicking "CUSTOMERS" opens the main vertical drop-down list (containing My Customers, Tariffs & Currencies, etc.).
 Secondary Level (Cascading): Hovering over "My Customers" (indicated by the small right-facing arrow) expands a second, nested menu to the right containing Accounts, Customers, Account Classes, Permissions, and Account Lookup.
