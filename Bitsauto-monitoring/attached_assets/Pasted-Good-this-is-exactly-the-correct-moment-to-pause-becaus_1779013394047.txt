“Everything is averaged into one number.”

Pros

Cons (serious in your case)

“We only decide when signals agree OR confidence is strong enough.”

Cons
Slightly more complex logic
Requires good confidence scoring discipline
Best for:
Multi-source operational intelligence systems (like yours)
