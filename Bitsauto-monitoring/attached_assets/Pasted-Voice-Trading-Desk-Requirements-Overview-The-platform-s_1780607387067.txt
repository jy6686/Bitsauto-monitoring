Voice Trading Desk Requirements

Overview

The platform should include a dedicated Voice Trading Desk module to manage voice wholesale operations, vendor negotiations, customer pricing, routing decisions, quality monitoring, and commercial performance from a single interface.

The Voice Trading Desk should act as the operational hub between:

* Vendors
* Customers
* Routing Team
* NOC Team
* Billing Team
* Management
