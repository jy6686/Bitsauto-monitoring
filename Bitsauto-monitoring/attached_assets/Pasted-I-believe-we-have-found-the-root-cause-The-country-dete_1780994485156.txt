For Call Governance and destination analytics, the country must always be determined from the CLD, not the CLI.

Example:

Similarly, for Eritrea:

CLD starts with Eritrea country code → Destination = Eritrea ✅
CLI may belong to a completely different country and must not affect destination classification ❌
