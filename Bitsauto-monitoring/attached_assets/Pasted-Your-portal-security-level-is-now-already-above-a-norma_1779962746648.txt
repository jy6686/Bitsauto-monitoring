Layer 2 — Data Scope Security

(CRITICAL)

Your biggest future risk is:

cross-tenant leakage.

You must enforce:

scope filtering

AT QUERY LEVEL,
not UI level.

Meaning:

WHERE client_id IN assignedClients

must exist server-side everywhere.

Never trust:

hidden buttons,
hidden pages,
frontend filtering.
