One thing I would change in Asterisk

Since you're open to changing Asterisk:

I would explicitly stamp the product on every governed call.

For example:

__BITSAUTO_PRODUCT=FC
__BITSAUTO_PRODUCT=BC
__BITSAUTO_PRODUCT=SC

and persist it into the governed call record.

Then Billing Verification never has to guess.

Instead of:

Find Product

the system already knows:

Call 12345
Product = FC

from the moment the call is created.

That is much safer than deriving product later from CLD patterns.

My conclusion

I would not consider automated invoicing, deal profitability, margin intelligence, customer billing reports, or revenue assurance "trusted" until:

Product Identity
+
Rate Repository
+
Rate Integrity Audit

exist and are authoritative.

The reports you've provided show that product prefixes materially affect rates, so product identity must become a first-class field across Asterisk → Sippy → CDR → P&L → Billing → Invoice. That's now a commercial control requirement, not merely a technical enhancement.
